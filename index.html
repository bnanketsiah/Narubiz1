Operations & Capacity

From Farm to Global Markets

Delivering high-quality Technically Specified Rubber (TSR) with state-of-the-art processing capabilities.

01

Raw Material Sourcing

We source cup lumps from our own farms and through technical agreements with 1,260+ local rubber farmers, ensuring fair pricing and providing technical assistance for higher yields.

02

Processing (1 Ton/Hr)

Raw latex coagulated into cup lumps is ground to extract water and chemicals. Currently running 24 hours a day with plans to expand to 5 tons/hour.

03

Product (TSR Grade 10)

Our Technically Specified Rubber is the essential material used globally to manufacture lorry tires, aeroplane tires, rubber bands, and engine seat supports.

04

Export & Value Addition

Exporting to Europe, Asia, and Africa via Sai Commodities. In the next two years, we will forward integrate into finished products like fan belts and water hoses for car manufacturers.
